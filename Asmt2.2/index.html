About the Fest

Welcome to the ultimate convergence of logic and artistry. TechVortex & CultFest brings together the brightest minds and most passionate performers from across the country. Whether you want to battle it out in intensive hackathons, showcase robust engineering projects, or dazzle on the cultural stage, there is a spotlight waiting just for you.

Why Participate?

  • National level validation and certification.
  • Cash prizes worth up to $5,000 across multiple categories.
  • Direct networking with top-tier industry professionals and talent scouts.
